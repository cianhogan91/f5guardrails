## Public
Information approved for public release.

## Internal
Information intended for employees, not the public. Most policies and guides fall here.

## Confidential
Information that could harm FinCorp or customers if exposed:
- Customer records
- Financial reporting not yet public
- Proprietary designs and architecture

## Restricted
Highest sensitivity:
- Authentication secrets (API keys, passwords)
- Customer PII and payment details
- Incident response details

## Safe-Chat reminder
Restricted data must never be entered into AI tools.
