# FinCorp Glossary (Internal)

## PII
Personally Identifiable Information — data that can identify an individual (e.g., SSN).

## PCI
Payment Card Industry — standards and concerns related to credit card data.

## RAG
Retrieval-Augmented Generation — retrieving approved context from a knowledge base and providing it to an LLM to ground answers.

## Guardrails
Controls that scan, block, redact, or otherwise constrain inputs/outputs.
