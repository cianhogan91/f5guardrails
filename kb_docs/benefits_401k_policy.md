## Eligibility
- Employees become eligible to enroll after 30 days of employment.
- Enrollment changes take effect the first payroll cycle after processing.

## Employer Match (FinCorp Match Policy)
- FinCorp matches *100% of employee contributions up to 4% of base salary*.
- Match is applied per pay period (not a year-end true-up for this demo).
- Match contributions are subject to plan rules and may change with annual benefits updates.

## Vesting
- Employee contributions: 100% vested immediately.
- Employer match: 100% vested after 12 months of employment.
